For grown-ups 🧑‍🤝‍🧑

Little Lights is a narrated tap-and-learn game for ages 2–6: colors and their meanings, the seven "rainbow body lights" (chakras), plants that grow above and below the ground, calm-breathing and "energy rivers" (meridians), and space. Every prompt is read aloud, so pre-readers can play on their own.

Progress saves on this device automatically — each child picks their animal and picks up where they left off. Levels adjust up and down on their own as your child answers.

Plant safety: the garden game teaches one golden rule — never eat any plant, berry, or mushroom without asking a grown-up first. Please reinforce it in real life!
